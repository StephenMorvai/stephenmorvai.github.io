Projects

Quantum Leap on Kongregate

Quantum Leap is a game where the player controls an electron on a hydrogen atom and gets
hit by photons which change the energy level of the electron. The player must try to avoid
getting knocked off the electron entirely. This project uses JavaScript, CSS, and HTML.


BlackBerry Playbook Educational App

A BlackBerry Playbook App developed with a partner based on criteria given by elementary school students.
The students could not decide on a single game so they gave us three mini games to develop. In the first
game the player helps a penguin jump to across the water by answering basic math problems. In the second
game the player guides an eagle through the air trying to avoid trees and other obstacles while chasing
mice and fish. In the last game the player helps a cat drop apples on a mouse and gets more apples by
answering math questions.
